WELCOME TO THE HPC PERFORMANCE GUIDE

This website is designed to be a comprehensive resource for students, researchers, and professionals interested in learning about version control with Git, collaborative development using GitHub, and high-performance computing (HPC) optimization techniques.

Whether you are new to these topics or looking to deepen your understanding, this platform provides structured content, practical tutorials, and real-world examples to help you build your skills and apply them effectively in your projects.
